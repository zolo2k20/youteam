WHAT MAKES YOUTEAM TALENT POOL UNIQUE?

93% of world’s contract developers are not freelancers

All freelance developers in the world combined constitute a mere 7% of the market. The overwhelming majority of engineers that work as a contractors for external clients are part of IT consulting firms or software development shops.

Good engineers want to choose jobs for themselves

The biggest problem of an IT consultancy is churn of engineers. The annual rate of those leaving is between 10 and 20%. The main reason is they don’t like the project they are working on. Good engineers don’t want to get stuck on a boring job - but it’s often hard to their firm to find new project for them quickly.

When you hire these engineers via YouTeam - everybody wins!

YouTeam makes it possible for these engineers to make themselves available to be hired for your new project. The result is a classic win-win-win situation: the company doesn’t loose the engineer and gets a new client, the engineers can choose themselves what they want to work on and you as a client get access to the previously concealed world largest pool of engineering talent.
